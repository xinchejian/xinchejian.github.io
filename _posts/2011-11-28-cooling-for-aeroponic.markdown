<p><!--:en-->The water in aeroponic box has been staying at 30 degree and it's BAD for the plants. With Michael's help, we designed a new cooling system for the water. Hopefully, this will keep the water at more comfortable 20 degrees for the lettuces. </p>
<p><br /><br /><center><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/72428EA9-3F11-45A7-8DC3-E469E332C67B2.jpg' border='0' width='480' width='500'style='margin:5px'></center><br /><br />
Hope to keep it at this temperature. </p>
<p><br /><br /><center><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/042D4A0E-A827-4778-AB3C-914C79AD31CD3.jpg' border='0' width='480' width='500'style='margin:5px'></center><br /><br />
The total system has 50L of water and mixed with the nutrient from Taobao.<br />
<br /><br /><center><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/A743EDED-E253-4B35-A3E7-8A6E5A1B97504.jpg' border='0' width='480' width='500'style='margin:5px'></center><br /><br />
<!--:--><!--:zh-->水植箱里的水以前可强大了，闲的没事就上30度，里面种的生菜成天跟洗桑拿似的，肯定长不好。幸亏有麦克大哥前来搭救，设计了新的冷却系统，希望这下子生菜们能在20度左右的水箱里爽歪歪了。</p>

希望这个温度能一直保持住。</p>
