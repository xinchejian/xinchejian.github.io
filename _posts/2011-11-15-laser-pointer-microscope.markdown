<p><!--:en-->A simple DIY laser microscope. </p>
<p>1. 300 mA laser pointer from Taobao for RMB 51. This thing is powerful and dangerous. Handle carefully!<br />
<br /><br /><center><a href='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/035F8607-D704-4F2D-8C23-DFAB07B0F8232.jpg'><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/035F8607-D704-4F2D-8C23-DFAB07B0F8232.jpg' border='0' width='500'style='margin:5px'></a></center><br /><br />
2. A small drop of water. I use water from the aeroponic tank. </p>
<p><br /><br /><center><a href='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/1E16A40A-5463-40C5-9F60-04D2C7401F443.jpg'><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/1E16A40A-5463-40C5-9F60-04D2C7401F443.jpg' border='0' width='500' style='margin:5px'></a></center><br /><br />

Aim the laser right through the droplet. Wear dark glasses while aiming and focusing. Even the reflection from this powerful laser can be dangerous! </p>

4. Awesome laser show with live microbes swimming inside that you can watch for hours!!!<br />

<p>1. 在淘宝上51元淘到的300 mA 激光笔。虽然便宜，但是是很强的激光。使用的时候要小心。<br />
<br /><br /><center><a href='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/035F8607-D704-4F2D-8C23-DFAB07B0F8232.jpg'><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/035F8607-D704-4F2D-8C23-DFAB07B0F8232.jpg' border='0' width='500'style='margin:5px'></a></center><br /></p>
<p>2. 一小滴的水。这里用的是水耕箱里面的营养液。</p>
<p><br /><br /><center><a href='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/1E16A40A-5463-40C5-9F60-04D2C7401F443.jpg'><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/1E16A40A-5463-40C5-9F60-04D2C7401F443.jpg' border='0' width='500' style='margin:5px'></a></center><br /><br />
<br /><br /><center><a href='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/3BA57AF7-6E1E-4AC8-AC9A-EEED098FCD0E4.jpg'><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/3BA57AF7-6E1E-4AC8-AC9A-EEED098FCD0E4.jpg' border='0' width='500' style='margin:5px'></a></center><br /> </p>
<p>3. 把激光对准水滴。这个激光非常的亮，对焦的时候建议带上太阳眼镜，保护眼睛。</p>
<p><br /><br /><center><a href='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/404205B3-CC2D-4AEB-9749-488F73F612685.jpg'><img src='http://xinchejian.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/404205B3-CC2D-4AEB-9749-488F73F612685.jpg' border='0' width='500' style='margin:5px'></a></center><br /></p>
<p>4. 很酷的激光投影，看到活生生的微生物在水里面游泳。</p>
