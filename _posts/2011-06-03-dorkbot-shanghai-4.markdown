<p>XinCheJian is proud to host <a href="http:&#47;&#47;dorkbot.org&#47;dorkbotshanghai&#47;">Dorkbot Shangahi<&#47;a> #4 on Sunday May 29th. <a href="http:&#47;&#47;en.wikipedia.org&#47;wiki&#47;Dorkbot">Dorkbot<&#47;a> is a group of affiliated organizations worldwide that sponsor grassroots meetings of artists, engineers, designers, scientists, inventors, and anyone else working under the very broad umbrella of electronic art. The dorkbot motto is "people doing strange things with electricity".<&#47;p></p>
<p>There were 4 artists who presented their work:<&#47;p></p>
<p><strong><a href="http:&#47;&#47;www.artlinkart.com&#47;en&#47;artist&#47;overview&#47;07cbsAt">Wu Juehui<&#47;a><&#47;strong> &#47;&#47; Juehui is a New Media Artist. In recent years, Wu Juehui operates on the potential interface between art and science, between body and media in close collaboration with research institutes for neural engineering, automatic control and computer graphics.<&#47;p></p>
